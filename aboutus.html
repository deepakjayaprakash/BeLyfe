Buisness Idea

  • BeLyfe is a startup that has aspiring goals.
  • BeLyfe aims to deliver an idea that, "Life is meant to be experienced as it is", with this we create. Video appications are abundant with social elements..
  • But BeLyfe is different than the rest because it's used to record memories and re-enact the whole day's events in one hour.


The Purpose behind it all

  • Memories are worth saving
  • Social networks have made the world more "connected" diaspora for all. But with this immense connection people have lost memories to these networks.
  • BeLyfe is "connecting" people with their memories, as memories are experiences worth preserving.

Concise list of features

  • Upload upto 1 minute of total of 60 cips per day
  • Video moments are automaticay arranged as per a few factors( Time, Date, Location).
  • 30 days of periodic use, the users avail a feature for unlocking the "movie" mode, where past archives are summed in as a movie.
  • The "movie mode" is 100% user customizable.
  • Highly customizable user profiles.
  • Unique login mechanism
  • Location will be tagged upon users permission once availed users can see through the locations of the video taken.
  • Highly customizable user profiles.
  • The locations will entail "Lyfe points"
  • Hand developed filters
  • Live edit on record
  • 720p and 1080p and above
  • High speed video rendering Dynamic Slo Mo
  • HDR Video
  • 3D Video Stablization
  • Auto Roll
